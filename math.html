I have a strong passion and love for math. I have been writing math competitions since grade 3 and have recieved recognition in many. My recent achievements(they weren't really too impressive but yea) include 4th place finish at the University of Toronto Undergraduate Math Contest, national first place & perfect score in the Canadian Math Kangaroo Contest,invitation to write the CMO, and a score of 89/100 in the Euclid Math Contest

Here are some resources/advice that may help when preparing for math contests. I'm not too good myself but I have some friends who are really good at math, a few of whom received recognition on naitonal and international levels.

  • Do past University of Waterloo math contests
  • Take some olympiads courses(i just heard about these never took any myself)
  • Check out cms website, which has contests like COMC, CMO, APMO, etc
  • Summer math program
  • Become part of the AOPS community!
